# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful environment for everyone regardless of experience level, gender, identity, race, religion, or background.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Using welcoming, inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what's best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or insults
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct that could be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Maintainers have the right to remove comments, commits, code, or other contributions that are not aligned to this Code of Conduct.
